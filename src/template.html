HEX BOIS

Build your empire of hexagons! Destroy other players in fast paced multiplayer battles. Manage your money, defenses and enemies as your empire grows!

With its simple yet addictive gameplay, HEX BOIS has reached over 500,000 players. Purchase hexagons to place on the board, making sure to balance your production of cashflow with your defenses.

Account system

HEX BOIS has its own accounts system built on Google's SSO. Players with an account are able to customize the colour of their hexagons as well as purchase 'skins' in the shop to add images to their hexagons. These purchases help the game's development. The account system also allows for fine tune moderation controls within the game as well as other cool features like switching devices during a match.

To keep the barrier of entry to play as low as possible, accounts are optional, players are encouraged to create one, but all gameplay is available to 'guests' and playing is as easy as picking a name and pressing play!

Mobile compatibility

With a modern, responsive website HEX BOIS fully supports the mobile gaming experience, all from within the browser, on the same website as desktop players. This means HEX BOIS is available to everyone, from the same codebase!

HEX BOIS is free to play and available immediately in-browser! Check it out hexbois.com

VOXORP

With 17 content updates since its release, Voxorp has accrued over 1,000,000 minutes of total play time. The game focuses on giving players tools to create their own world in Voxorp's massive open multiplayer world.

The game is built with a custom physics and networking engine developed from scratch for Voxorp. This gives the game the capabilities to run such a large game world, synced between players from all around the globe.

Voxorp has three main elements of gameplay

  • Spaceships. As you progress through the game your ship grows and provides more opportunity to customize its functionality with 'modules'
  • Base building. Using the resources you collect in the game, you can create a base to claim your part of the map. Bases are complex, they require their own power system and defenses
  • Alliances. The robust alliance system allows players from around the world to collaborate on designing a base, defending their territory and battling it out with other alliances in massive space battles

Voxorp is free to play and available immediately in-browser! Check it out voxorp.com
